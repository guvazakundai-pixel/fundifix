Our Story

Built From Real Experience, Not Theory

My name is Ashley, and Repair Hub was created from a real and frustrating experience.

After my phone was stolen, I was left trying to get back to normal life as quickly as possible. I depended on my phone for everything — communication, work, banking, and daily tasks. During that stressful time, I searched for an affordable repair option and visited a local repair shop.

I was told I would receive an original replacement screen at a much lower price than usual. I trusted what I was told because I needed my phone fixed quickly and couldn't afford delays or higher costs.

Unfortunately, the repair did not meet the quality that was promised. That experience revealed a bigger problem: people don't really know who is actually qualified, whether parts are genuine, if pricing is fair, or what quality of service they'll receive.

Repair Hub was built to change that.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Repair Hub.

How does Kundai work?

Kundai is our AI repair assistant. Simply tell her what's wrong with your device — like "my screen is cracked" or "my phone won't charge" — and she'll help diagnose the problem, estimate costs, and connect you with the best technician near you.

How do I find a technician?

Use our search directory to filter technicians by city, device brand, repair type, and verification status. Each profile shows ratings, reviews, specialties, response times, and contact details.

Are technicians verified?

Yes. We have three verification levels: Verified Technician (ID and skill verified), Trusted Technician (consistently high ratings), and Top Rated Technician (top performers with extensive track records). Look for the verification badges on each profile.

How much do repairs cost?

Use our Pricing Guide to get fair cost estimates for any repair across all major phone brands. Prices are based on real market data collected in Harare to help you avoid being overcharged.

How do I become a technician?

Click "Become a Tech" and fill out the application form with your shop details, experience, and specialties. Our team will review your application and verify your credentials before listing you on the platform.

Can I leave reviews?

Absolutely. After completing a repair, you can rate your technician on quality, speed, communication, pricing, and professionalism. Reviews help other customers make informed decisions and help technicians build their reputation.
